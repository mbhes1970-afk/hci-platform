Requirement What it means for your product
NEN 7510

Information security in healthcare		 Mandatory for all healthcare organisations. Your product must fit within a NEN 7510-certified environment or hold certification itself. Audit trail: 4-9 months to certification.
GDPR / AVG

Patient data processing		 DPA appointment, DPIA required, verwerkersovereenkomst mandatory before any pilot. EU hosting required — no exceptions for patient data.
NIS2

Network & information security		 In force 2025. Healthcare organisations must demonstrate security controls for all vendors with system access. Your compliance status is a procurement criterion.
WGBO

Medical treatment contracts		 Patient information obligations. Any product touching patient communication must demonstrate WGBO alignment — relevant for education, discharge, consent tools.
EU AI Act

AI classification		 AI-assisted medical tools may be classified as high-risk or prohibited. Classification must be determined before market entry — not after.

Market Drivers

Why now is the right moment for healthcare technology

Three structural forces are creating demand for technology in Dutch healthcare that didn't exist five years ago.

Workforce shortage & burnout
The Netherlands has a structural shortage of 100,000+ healthcare workers by 2030. Technology that reduces administrative burden, enables remote care and automates communication is a workforce strategy, not a luxury.
"This is the EU equivalent of the US HCAHPS driver — but it applies to every vendor, not just patient engagement tools."
NIS2 + NEN 7510 compliance pressure
From 2025, NIS2 requires healthcare organisations to demonstrate active information security controls. Any vendor with system access is now a procurement risk factor. Certified and compliant vendors have an advantage.
"Compliance is not a barrier — it is your differentiator if you have it and your competitor doesn't."
Digital sovereignty post-Kiteworks
The Dutch parliament officially confirmed CLOUD Act exposure for US-owned tools. Healthcare CISOs are re-evaluating every American vendor in their stack. European alternatives with on-premise options have a structural advantage.
"This is documented in Kamerstukken. It is not a sales argument — it is a fact."
Health literacy & patient communication gap
25-30% of the Dutch population has limited health literacy. WGBO requires adequate patient information. Video-based education, multilingual tools and digital consent platforms address a legal obligation — not a nice-to-have.
"Frame your product around the legal obligation. That is the conversation that gets past procurement."
