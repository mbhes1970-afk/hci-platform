Six Dimensions

What we're actually measuring

Each dimension has 3–4 questions. Rate yourself 0–10 on each. Together they give a composite score that reflects real EU market readiness — not surface-level optimism.

01
ICP Clarity
Can you describe your ideal European customer in one sentence — sector, company size, job title, specific pain? EU enterprise buyers require precision.
3 questions · Target definition · EU buyer profile · Decision-maker mapping
02
Regulatory Compliance
Which EU regulations apply to your product, and what is your current compliance status? GDPR, NIS2, AI Act, CRA, DORA — each has commercial implications.
4 questions · Applicable regulations · Data architecture · Certification · Roadmap
03
Product-Market Fit EU
Does your product solve a problem that EU buyers recognise — in language they use? US metrics may need reframing around compliance, sovereignty or workforce efficiency.
3 questions · Problem recognition · EU value drivers · Competitive landscape
04
GTM Model
Direct, indirect or hybrid? Your channel model depends on product type, price point, sector and market maturity. The wrong model burns introductions.
3 questions · Channel strategy · Sales cycle · First-customer plan
05
Team Readiness
Who manages the EU relationship on your side? EU buyers expect continuity, local language capability and someone with decision-making authority in their timezone.
3 questions · EU point of contact · Language · Leadership commitment
06
Financial Runway
EU enterprise sales cycles run 6–18 months. Do you have the runway to sustain a 24-month engagement? Budget clarity protects both sides.
4 questions · Runway duration · Budget allocation · Revenue independence · Board sign-off
